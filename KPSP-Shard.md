> Managed by ExPod. Do not modify above the Session Notes line.

## Objective
- **Mission:** Codify a proven organizational management theory into an accessible, actionable publication.
- **Deliverable:** Two publication formats — a PDF book (source for print publication on Amazon and digital distribution) and a companion website presenting the framework. The PDF is the canonical source; the site is the public face.
- **Intent:** Transform a proven personal management system into a published book others can buy, read, and adopt — with a web presence for discovery and ongoing reference

## Scope
Master reference documentation (9 core docs), evidence collection (stories, case studies), published guide drafting and editing, allegory system (Plot of Land, Fiefdom, Lord/Scribe/Castle/Village), PDF book production (Amazon print + digital), companion website for public presentation. Out of scope: software tooling for the framework, consulting services, certification programs
